Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 6, 2026 ¬∑ Last updated: April 6, 2026

Luminic Light Meter ("we", "our", or "the app") is a light meter application for iOS developed by Felix Swensson. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle.

Summary

Luminic Light Meter does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs.

Camera Access

Luminic Light Meter requests access to your device's camera for one purpose only: to measure the brightness of the scene in front of the lens so it can calculate exposure values (EV), aperture, and shutter speed.

  • The camera feed is processed entirely on your device, in real time.
  • No images, video, or frames are recorded, saved to disk, uploaded, or sent anywhere.
  • The camera is only active while the app is open and in the foreground.

You can revoke camera access at any time in Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Camera ‚Üí Luminic. The app will not function as a light meter without camera access.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Location data
  • Photos or videos
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Contacts, calendar, microphone, or any other device data

Third Parties

Luminic Light Meter does not include any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or cloud services. Your data never leaves your device because the app never sends data anywhere.

Children's Privacy

Luminic Light Meter does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
