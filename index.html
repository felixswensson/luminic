How to Use

1. Launch Luminic Light Meter and grant camera permission when asked.

2. Point your iPhone at the scene you want to meter.

3. Tap MODE to switch between Aperture Priority (A) and Shutter Priority (S).

4. Use the ISO buttons to set your film/sensor speed.

Troubleshooting

Camera not working? Check Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Camera ‚Üí Luminic and make sure access is enabled.

Readings look off? Luminic Light Meter uses the iPhone camera sensor, which is calibrated differently than a dedicated incident meter. For critical work, calibrate against a known reference and apply a fixed offset in your head.

App is shaky? Cinematic stabilization is enabled by default. If you're seeing jitter, try switching lenses or restarting the app.
